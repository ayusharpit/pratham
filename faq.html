What is Pratham and what is the purpose of the event?

Pratham is a event which is organized by ATL DAV KN members in DAV Public School, Koyla Nagar Dhanabd. There will be a three day workshop from 7th to 9th of April. There will be 3 events during this period. Any student from class 5 to 12 of DAV KN can participate in the events.

When and where will Pratham be held?

From 7th april and it will happen at School campus

There will be guest talks, workshops and exciting events many more.
